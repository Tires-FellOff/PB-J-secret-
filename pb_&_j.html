PB & J Recipe!

Learn how to make PB & J!

Instructions:

  • 2 slices of bread.
  • Peanut butter.
  • Jelly (Grape Jelly).

Instructions:

  1. Lay the 2 bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on the first slice.
  3. Spread jelly on the second slice.
  4. Put both slices together with the toppings facing each other.
